Piggies In A Blanket

A description that is commonly used to refer to any type of sausage covered with a bread or pastry dough wrap. Foods such as small cocktail wieners wrapped in a baked pie dough, hot dogs in a bun, frankfurter slices in a pita wrap, a sausage wrapped in a pancake, or a corndog consisting of a wiener encased in fried breading are all examples of foods considered "Pigs in a Blanket." The original version of this food item which came from Germany consisted of rice enclosed in a cabbage leaf.
